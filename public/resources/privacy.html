Data we collect

We collect the following data required to operate the service:
  • Account ID, username, and email if available from your signup method;
  • Session ID, stored on your computer via cookies;
  • Various statistics about your site usage; (signup method used, time of registration, unlocked Vukkies, opened boxes, redeemed codes, etc.)

Tracking

We use Cloudflare to protect our service from attacks, so you are subject to their privacy policy as well. You are a "End User" in that document.
We do not store any other cookies, and we do not embed tracking or analytics code in the Service.

Please note that when you use one of the login providers, their Terms of Service and Privacy Policy apply while you are there.

How we use your data

Your Account ID, Session ID, and username is used to identify you. Your Account ID may be logged to us if it is detected that your account is bypassing restrictions set by us.
Your email is used to identify you in support requests and send you important notifications.
Statistics are used for the statistics page and the leaderboards.
We log the time when you sign up and what login method you used to see if a login method is worth keeping. This log does not contain any other information.

Everything we collect is stored on our servers and is only used for service operation.
We do not have advertising partners, and we do not give your data to any third parties other than those mentioned in the "Tracking" section.

Your rights

No matter where you are, we at LIT Devs believe you should have control over your data. You can:
  • View your data, as stored on our servers, by going to https://vukkybox.com/stats.
  • Update your data by going to your profile page and clicking on your name or profile picture.
  • Move your data to a different login provider by contacting us. Please note that your e-mail must be sent from the e-mail address you signed up with (see "View your data".)
  • Delete your data by contacting us in the same way as moving your data, or going to https://vukkybox.com/delete.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time, but you will be notified on the Vukkybox website and by e-mail.
These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
